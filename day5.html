Day 5: TeamLabs & Fun Day

Itinerary

🧖‍♀️ 9:00 AM – 10:30 AM – Spa time (try Thermae-Yu in Shinjuku)
🚿 Return to Airbnb to get ready for the day

🎨 1:00 PM – 2:30 PM – Visit teamLab Planets in Toyosu
🍣 2:30 PM – 3:30 PM – Grab lunch at or near Toyosu 🏎️ 4:00 PM – 5:30 PM – Mario Street Karting adventure (TBD)

🌆 6:30 PM – 8:00 PM – Explore Shinjuku:
🛍️ Shop Lumine EST and Don Quijote
📸 Snap a pic with the Godzilla Head in Kabukichō

🌃 9:00 PM – 10:30 PM – Visit Roppongi Hills Sky Deck or Tokyo Midtown
🍽️ Optional dinner at Soul Food House (Black-owned in Azabu-Juban)
🏠 11:30 PM – Return to Airbnb & rest

Notes

Roppongi is rich with indie galleries and diaspora-friendly nightlife. For late vibes, check out Motown House or Propaganda Bar for funk, soul & R&B.

Food Ideas – Day 5

  • 🥞 Breakfast: Café Aaliya or Bam Bi Coffee (Shinjuku)
  • 🍱 Dinner: Gyukatsu Kyoto Katsugyu, Tonkatsu Wako, or Shinjuku Ark Lounge
  • 🍡 Treats: Snowy Village, Press Butter Sand, or Cow Cow Ice Cream
