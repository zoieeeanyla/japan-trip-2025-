Day 8: Departure Day

Itinerary

🧳 8:00 AM – 9:30 AM – Light breakfast & final packing at the Airbnb
☕ 9:30 AM – 10:15 AM – Walk to Kameari Park for fresh air & photos
    • Bonus: Take pics with Ryotsu Kankichi statues from the manga KochiKame
    • Option: visit Kameari Station shopping area (local shops & cafés)
🍱 10:15 AM – 11:00 AM – Brunch at a nearby café or diner
    • Try Komeda’s Coffee or Gusto Kameari for a casual local meal
🛍️ 11:00 AM – 11:45 AM – Last-minute snacks/souvenirs at Don Quijote Aoto or Ito-Yokado Kameari
    • Can also stop by FamilyMart or 7-Eleven for airport snacks or omiyage
🚗 12:00 PM – Private car or train to Haneda Airport (HND)
✈️ 3:15 PM – Flight home

Departure Notes

  • ✅ Make sure all passports and boarding passes are ready
  • 🧼 Check Airbnb for forgotten items before leaving
  • 🎁 Pack omiyage (souvenirs) properly to avoid squishing
  • 💤 Sleep on the plane if you can – recovery mode!
