Day 6: Ueno, Akihabara & Asakusa

Itinerary

🐼 11:00 AM – 1:00 PM – Visit Ueno Zoo and walk through Ueno Park
🎮 1:30 PM – 3:30 PM – Explore Akihabara – arcades, anime, tech heaven
🏮 4:00 PM – 6:00 PM – Visit Senso-ji Temple and shop along Nakamise-dori

🍵 Optional: Kimono + tea ceremony at Maikoya or Camellia Flower Teahouse
🍡 Optional Snacks: melon pan, taiyaki, mochi

🍽️ Dinner: Flexible – stay in Asakusa or grab meals near the Airbnb
🏠 8:00 PM – Return to Airbnb & rest

Notes

Some group members may break off for a Fast & Furious adventure (time TBD).

Asakusa is a peaceful and historic neighborhood – a great place to reflect and take beautiful photos. Keep an eye out for Black artisan pop-ups and handmade accessories near Nakamise-dori.

Food Ideas – Day 6

  • 🍱 Lunch: Asakusa Chaya Tabanenoshi or street food along Nakamise-dori
  • 🍣 Dinner: Flexible in Asakusa or convenience meals near Airbnb
  • 🍡 Treats: Ben Zai Ten (mochi) or a snack run at 7-Eleven
