Day 1: Monday, May 26 – Arrival in Tokyo, Visit Shibuya

Itinerary

  • ✈️ 3:50 PM – Arrive at Haneda Airport (HND)
  • 🚗 4:30 PM – Private car service to Airbnb
  • 🚆 6:00 PM – 6:45 PM – Explore Harajuku's Takeshita Street (snacks, crepes, photo ops)
  • 🌆 7:00 PM – 8:00 PM – Head to Shibuya: see Shibuya Crossing & Shibuya Sky
  • 🍱 8:00 PM – Dinner in Shibuya (e.g., Ichiran Ramen, Uobei Sushi, or Genki Izakaya)
  • 🏠 9:30 PM – Return to Airbnb & rest

Notes

Explore Harajuku & Shibuya with pride — bold style is celebrated. Keep an eye out for Black-owned pop-ups, and check Loft or Shibuya 109 for diverse haircare brands.

🍽️ Food Ideas

  • 🥞 Breakfast: Flippers (Shibuya) or The Little Bakery Tokyo (Omotesando)
  • 🍱 Dinner: Ichiran Ramen, Uobei Sushi, or Genki Izakaya (Shibuya)
  • 🍡 Treats: Harajuku crepes or convenience store sweets
