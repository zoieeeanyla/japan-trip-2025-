Explore Harajuku & Shibuya with pride — bold style is celebrated. Keep an eye out for Black-owned pop-ups, and check Loft or Shibuya 109 for diverse haircare brands.

🥞 Breakfast: Flippers (Shibuya) or The Little Bakery Tokyo (Omotesando)

🍱 Dinner: Ichiran Ramen, Uobei Sushi, or Genki Izakaya (Shibuya)

🍡 Treats: Harajuku crepes or convenience store sweets
