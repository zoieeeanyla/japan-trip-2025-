Day 3: Mt. Fuji Day Trip

🚐 8:00 AM – Depart Tokyo via private van
🗻 10:30 AM – 1:00 PM – Explore Lake Kawaguchi, Chureito Pagoda, and nearby viewpoints
🍱 1:00 PM – 2:00 PM – Lunch with Mt. Fuji views (Hoto Fudo recommended)
🛍️ 2:15 PM – 3:15 PM – Optional stop at Gotemba Premium Outlets
🚐 3:30 PM – 6:00 PM – Return to Tokyo
🚆 6:30 PM – Arrive in Ikebukuro
🎮 6:45 PM – 8:00 PM – Explore Sunshine City: Pokémon Center Mega Tokyo, Namco arcade, optional Sunshine Aquarium
🍣 8:00 PM – 9:00 PM – Dinner
🛍️ 9:00 PM – 9:30 PM – Quick browse at Tokyu Hands or Don Quijote
🎒 9:30 PM – 10:00 PM – Return to Airbnb & rest — the kids will crash happy!

Food Ideas – Day 3

  • 🍱 Lunch: Hoto Fudo (Udon near Mt. Fuji)
  • 🍣 Dinner: Ichiran Ramen or Sushi-Ro in Ikebukuro
  • 🍡 Treats: Dessert in Sunshine City mall or Pokémon Café pop-up
