Day 4: Split Day - Kyoto / Odaiba

Group A – Kyoto / Osaka Itinerary

✈️ 6:00 AM – Depart Tokyo via Shinkansen to Kyoto
⛩️ 8:15 AM – 11:00 AM – Explore Fushimi Inari Shrine & Gion
🍜 11:30 AM – Lunch in Kyoto (ramen, udon, or bento)
🚆 12:30 PM – Local train to Osaka
🏯 1:00 PM – 4:30 PM – Visit Osaka Castle, explore Dotonbori
🍢 5:00 PM – Street food dinner (takoyaki, okonomiyaki)
🚄 6:30 PM – Shinkansen back to Tokyo
🏠 9:30 PM – Return to Airbnb & rest

Group B – Odaiba / Tokyo Itinerary

🎮 10:30 AM – Travel to Odaiba
🎢 11:00 AM – 2:00 PM – Play at Joypolis indoor amusement park
🍣 2:30 PM – Lunch at Aqua City Odaiba (casual options)
🧑‍🚀 3:30 PM – 5:00 PM – Choose from:
- Miraikan Science Museum
- DiverCity + Gundam Statue
- Legoland Discovery Center (for younger kids)
🌅 5:30 PM – Relax at Odaiba Beach for sunset & photos
🏠 7:00 PM – Return to Airbnb & rest

Food Ideas – Day 4

Group A – Kyoto

  • 🍱 Lunch: Tsujiya Shijogokomachiten or Tendon Makino Teramachi
  • 🍣 Dinner: Kura Sushi (buffet) or Ayam Ya Karasuma (halal ramen)

Group B – Odaiba

  • 🍔 Lunch: MOS Burger or Aqua City food court
  • 🍱 Dinner (Shinjuku): Teppan Baby, Dancing Crab, or Tonkatsu Wako
